Futurpay / Dunyapay / Fixopay - Business Model

Executive Summary

Futurpay /Dunyapay / Fixopay is a joint venture between YH and AM, providing crypto payment processing solutions for forex/CFD platforms. The company combines YH's technical expertise with AM's market presence to offer a competitive, compliant, and comprehensive payment solution.

Key Partners

  • YH (50% ownership)
    • Technology & architecture expertise
    • Infrastructure support
  • AM (50% ownership)
    • First major client
    • Business development partner
  • Technology Partners
    • Fireblocks (custody)
    • Binance & Bybit (exchanges)
    • Sumsub (KYC)
    • Elliptic (monitoring)

Value Propositions

  • Competitive pricing
    • 0.5% standard commission rate
    • Volume-based pricing tiers
  • Comprehensive Solution
    • Support for 10 main cryptocurrencies
    • Seamless deposits & withdrawals
    • Advanced admin panel with detailed reporting
  • Security & Compliance
    • Reliable infrastructure via Fireblocks
    • Strong compliance framework
    • Full KYC/AML Compliance

Customer Segments

  • Primary: Forex/CFD Platforms
    • Starting with AM
    • Similar trading platforms
  • Future Segments
    • Other financial services
    • Global reach (no geographic limits)

Customer Relationships

  • Direct B2B relationships
  • Dedicated support team
  • Self-service admin panel
  • Technical integration support
  • Compliance assistance

Key Activities

  • Crypto payment processing
  • Currency conversion to USDC
  • KYC/AML compliance
  • Transaction monitoring
  • System maintenance
  • Client onboarding

Key Resources
